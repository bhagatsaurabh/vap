Audio Source

Generates a constant metronome indefinitely at a specified BPM.

Inputs

trigger (event)
Triggers the metronome upon receiving an event on this terminal.
gain (audioparam)
Controls gain of audio output.
detune (audioparam)
Controls tune adjustment of audio output.
playback-rate (audioparam)
Controls output playback rate.

Outputs

out (audio)
The audio output.

Properties

Auto Whether to start the metronome automatically or wait for a trigger
Frequency The frequency of the generated clicks
BPM Beats per minute
